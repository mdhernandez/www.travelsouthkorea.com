Visit Seoul!

Seoul is the capital and largest metropolis in South Korea. It is home to over half of the South Korean Population and is known for being a tourist hotspot. Seoul is a must-visit city for anyone interested in South Korea.

Key Attractions!:

War Memorial of Korea
A site to memorialize the military history of Korea and remember the soldiers from many different countries that fought in the Korean War.
Gwanghwamun Square
An iconic plaza in Seoul that features museums, statues, a performing center, and the main royal palace in Korean history, Gyeongbok Palace.
Seoul Tower
A communication and observation tower located on Namsan Mountain, marking the highest point in Seoul.

Transportation:

Subway Information

Seoul has one of the largest and most modern subway systems in the world. Noted for its clean appearance and peaceful atmosphere, the Seoul Metropolitan Subway is the preferred transportation choice of both locals and tourists alike!

Bus Information

In addition to the efficient subway system, Seoul also offers convenient Bus Travel options. Buses in Seoul are classified by color to make traveling throughout the city efficient and stress-free. You can find more information on buses by clicking the above link.

Intercity and Express Buses

Seoul also features bus terminals that allow convenient and comfortable travel across the country. This allows travelers and locals to explore the country conveniently and cost-efficiently.

T-Money Travel Card

Traveling in Seoul and throughout other parts of South Korea is made easier with the T-money travel card. The T-money card is a rechargeable payment card that can be used for almost any public transportation method in Seoul and can also be used in Busan and other locations throughout Korea.
