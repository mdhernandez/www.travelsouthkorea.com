Visit Busan!

Busan is South Korea's Second largest city with a population of about 3.6 million people. Busan is located on the southeastern-most tip of the Korean peninsula and is the largest port city in Korea. Busan is also home to Korea's largest beach and longest river. Busan also proudly owns the world's largest department store.

Key Attractions!:

Haeundae Beach
One of the most famous beaches in Korea. With white sand, clean water, and many cultural curiosities nearby, Haeundae Beach is a must-see for people visiting Busan.
Haedong Yonggung Temple
A traditional Buddhist temple located on a cliff overlooking the sea. Haedong Yonggung Temple has breathtaking sights to behold. This temple offers a unique experience no traveler to Busan should miss.
Gamcheon Culture Village
A traditional and colorful village located on a hill. Filled with art, wall murals, and street stands, this village offers a unique experience for those looking for a different perspective on Korea.

Hanaro Card

Busan offers a useful travel card called the Hanaro Card that makes getting around the city even easier. The Hanaro card can be used to pay for subways, buses, taxis and other travel methods. If you plan on moving around Busan a lot, you should consider using a Hanaro Card. For more information on the Hanaro card and other travel cards used in Busan, click the link above this section.
