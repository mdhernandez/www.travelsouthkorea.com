Here at Visit Korea!™, we have a small but dedicated staff. Each member is dedicated to providing the best service for our customers. This website was comissioned by the Republic of Korea's Department of Tourism for the purpose promoting tourism to Korea. Each member of our staff has experienced Korean culture firsthand and we are passionate about helping others experience it as well.


Kristie Kim

Kristie Kim founded Visit Korea!™ in 2015 with the hope of encouraging people to experience the culture that is such a big part of her life. Ms. Kim lived in Seoul, South Korea for the first 18 years of her life before attending UC Berkeley. Now she splits her time between Korea and the United States, promoting tourism between the two countries.

Hyun-Woo Sohn

Hyun-Woo Sohn currently works as a professor of culture and media at one of South Korea's top universities, Korea University. Professor Sohn is fluent in Korean, English, and Mandarin Chinese and often provides translating services for this company and for Korea University. Professor Sohn is a specialist Korean culture and world culture.

Jennifer Khan

Jennifer Khan graduated from SUNY Albany in 2006 and became a qualified Travel Agent in 2007. She is an experienced travel agent who is known for her customer satisfaction.

Michael Hernandez

Michael Hernandez graduated from Stony Brook University with a Bachelor's Degree in Science. He has attended Korea University for a semester and grew to love Korean culture. He is now a computer programmer who works to keep this website running efficiently.
